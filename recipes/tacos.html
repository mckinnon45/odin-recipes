Fabulous Beef Tacos with Pico de Gallo

These are some of the best tacos you'll ever find on the planet. Made with freshly fried corn tortillas, and sprinkled onion and cilantro makes this dish a must have.

Ingredients needed

  • 12 4.5" Corn Tortillas
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 tbs Coriander
  • Lime Wedges
  • 6 oz Diced Tomatoes
  • 4 oz Diced Onion
  • 1 tbs Ground Cumin
  • Cheese
  • Shredded Cilantro Leaves

Steps Needed

  1. Brown the beef and lightly season with the ground cumin and coriander during cooking.
  2. Fry the tortillas
  3. Put the beef on bottom of the tortillas, cheese next so it will melt on the hot beef and bond them together.
  4. Decorate rest of taco with the toppings

  5. ENJOY!
