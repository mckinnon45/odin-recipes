Lemonade Shake-Up

Delicious Lemonade Shake Up

This sweet treat will be the icing on the cake of a hot summer day. Will remind you of the County Fair

Ingredients needed

  • 32 oz cup
  • 1 Lemon
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Water
  • Ice

Steps to make the Lemonade

  1. Cut Lemon in half
  2. Squeeze Lemon juice into 32 oz cup. place the squeezed lemon halves into the cup as well
  3. Put 1/2 cup granulated sugar into cup
  4. Fill cup 3/4 full with ice
  5. Fill cup 3/4 full with water
  6. Stir the drink vigorously until sugar is disolved
  7. Top off with water
